Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a recipe originating from the Italian city of Bologna. It consists of pasta with a thick, slow-cooked sauce made with minced meat, tomato, onion, carrots, and celery, among other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 100g of carrots
  • 1 branch of celery
  • 50g of onion
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 250g minced meat(beff and pork)
  • 375g tomato passata(or fried tomato sauce)
  • 60 ml of white wine or sherry
  • 60 ml of water
  • Shall
  • Ground black pepper
  • 150 g of spaghetti

Steps

  1. We chop the carrots
  2. We chop the celery
  3. We chop the onion
  4. We fry the onion
  5. We add the carrot and celery
  6. Add the garlic and meat and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Add the passata and cook for 10 more minutes.
  8. Add the wine and water and let it reduce.
  9. We cover and cook for 1 hour.
  10. We cook the pasta, add it to the pan and serve.
